# Figure 14-1 Time to Dermatologic Event by Treatment Group

## Figure Description

Kaplan-Meier plot showing Product-Limit Survival Function Estimates for time to first dermatologic event by treatment group.

- **X-axis:** Time to Dermatologic Event or End of Study (range 0 to 200 days)
- **Y-axis:** Survival Probability (range 0 to 1)
- **Treatment groups:** Placebo (solid line), Xanomeline High Dose (dotted line), Xanomeline Low Dose (dashed line)
- **Logrank p<.0001**

The Placebo group maintains a substantially higher survival probability throughout the study period (~0.65 at end of study), while both Xanomeline dose groups show markedly lower survival probabilities, indicating higher rates of dermatologic events. The Xanomeline High Dose and Low Dose curves are similar, both declining to below 0.2 by approximately day 100.
